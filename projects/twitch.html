Personal Project: Twitch Overlays

The creation and and hobbies as an artist

Overview

This project represents a pivotal step towards realizing my dream of generating passive income as a resourceful college student. Motivated by a desire to leverage my creativity and technical skills, I embarked on the journey of creating my first listing of streaming overlays tailored for platforms like Twitch. Over the course of four months, I meticulously crafted a comprehensive package of assets designed to enhance the visual appeal and functionality of streaming channels. This included adjustable assets, pre-made screens, animated screens, customizable background screens, static screens, and panels, catering to the diverse needs of content creators.

Background and Purpose

Utilizing accessible tools such as my iPad, Canva, and Photoshop, I immersed myself in the process of design and customization, exploring various techniques to bring my vision to life. Despite the challenges posed by limited resources and time constraints, I remained dedicated to refining my creations and delivering a high-quality product. Through this project, I not only honed my skills in graphic design and digital artistry but also gained valuable experience in entrepreneurship and marketing. It served as a testament to my ingenuity and determination to carve out opportunities for myself, even in the face of financial constraints. Ultimately, this project represents more than just a venture into the realm of passive income; it embodies my passion for creativity, my commitment to personal growth, and my unwavering drive to pursue my dreams, no matter the obstacles.
